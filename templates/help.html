Is Bus Ticker real-time? No, unfortunately the City of Hamilton does not released real-time bus location feeds for the HSR. We urge you to write your representative at council and let them know real-time bus feeds are important to you!

How accurate are the bus times? The times that are shown on Bus Ticker are based on the official schedule released by the HSR. Actual time of arrival may vary based on traffic conditions and other considerations.

How do I install Bus Ticker? There is nothing to install! You simply go to Bus Ticker on your Android, iOS device and you can start using Bus Ticker immediately. You can add Bus Ticker to your home screen to make it easier to find. For instruction on how to do this for your device see the next question on this page.

How do I add Bus Ticker to my iPad or iPhone home screen? Simply go to Bus Ticker in Safari and tap then select 'Add to Home Screen' from the menu.

How do I add Bus Ticker to my Android home screen? First go to Bus Ticker in your browser and bookmark it. Then drag a bookmark widget to your home screen and select Bus Ticker when you are prompted to choose a bookmark.

I found a bug, who do I tell? The best way to let me know about a bug is to tweet me: @gsmaverick, or send me an email: gavin.schulz@gmail.com. Hopefully I'll be able to fix the bug in a reasonably short timeframe.
